Caribbean Examination Council Preparation Tips

Overview

Our tutoring process begins with an assessment test in the area in which you wish to be tutored. The test assists in assessing your skills and understanding level to allow us to better cater our tutoring at a pace and with a focus on the area in which you need to build your knowledge.

We then offer progress tests to evaluate your progress and better prepare you for the exam. We also offer mock examinations, which are timed to the actual examination to better prepare you for success on your examination day.
