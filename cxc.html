Tutoring Subjects and Topics

  • English A

    Understanding
    (a) understand meaning conveyed in reading, through word choice, grammar, punctuation and paragraphing, and to obtain information accurately;
    (b) grasp insights from reading literature;
    (c) evaluate opinions expressed in various forms.
    Expression
    (a) communicate factual information clearly, concisely, and adequately in giving written instructions, reports, summaries, and expositions in appropriate language;
    (b) give aesthetic satisfaction to others in personal, creative and imaginative language;
    (c) communicate personal opinion clearly and cogently in language which persuades or dissuades effectively.
